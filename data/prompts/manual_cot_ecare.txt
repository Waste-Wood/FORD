Q: There is a light rain today. What happened as a result? Answer Choices: (A) The roots of many plants are not moistened by rain. (B) Tourists have seen many ripples.
A: Light rain may not be enough to penetrate the soil deeply and reach the roots of many plants, which can cause the roots to remain dry. Therefore, the answer is (A).
